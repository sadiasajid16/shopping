Indica

This type of cannabis is commonly taken by those who want to sink into a state of total relaxation in every limb. This reduces stress and anxiety due to its soothing and calming high. Because of its relaxing effects, it's suggested to use this type of cannabis on a day where you have nothing to do or for people who have trouble sleeping, be it due to insomnia, pain or other associated sleep issues.

Shop Indica

Sativa

Contrary to the deep of body relaxation that comes with the use of Indica strains, sativas are more known for providing an energizing high that can assist those in reducing pain and anxiety. This is more of a mild cerebral high in terms of how and where you will feel the effects and it is more common than recommended to use this during the day.

Shop Sativa

Hybrid strains are just that - they combine the best-focused high effects of sativas with the bodily relaxation of Indicas.
